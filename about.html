About the App

The Psion Time Machine is a web-based emulator and archive that brings classic Psion handheld devices back to life. Dive into the world of vintage computing and experience the charm of early mobile technology.

Features

  • Emulates Psion Series 3a, 3c, and other models
  • Runs original Psion software in your browser
  • Preserves digital history with interactive archives
  • Open-source and community-driven
